📋 Cancellation Policy at a Glance

The refund you receive depends on how far in advance of departure you cancel. The earlier you cancel, the more you get back.

When You Cancel Refund Cancellation Fee Status
More than 48 hours before departure 100% of fare ₹0 Full Refund
24 – 48 hours before departure 75% of fare 25% of fare Partial Refund
0 – 24 hours before departure 50% of fare 50% of fare Half Refund
After departure time ₹0 100% of fare No Refund

* Convenience fee (₹20) and GST are non-refundable in all cases. Refund is calculated on the base seat fare only.

✅ How to Cancel Your Booking

Step 1 — Go to My Bookings

Log in to your BusMate account and navigate to "My Bookings" from the navigation bar.

Step 2 — Find Your Booking

Locate the booking you want to cancel. Only Confirmed bookings with a future departure date can be cancelled.

Step 3 — Click Cancel Booking

Click the "Cancel Booking" button. A confirmation prompt will appear. Once confirmed, the cancellation is immediate and cannot be undone.

Step 4 — Refund is Processed

Your refund is calculated automatically and initiated immediately. It will reflect in your original payment method within 5–7 working days.

⚠️ Important Rules

  • Cancellations are only possible for Confirmed bookings (payment completed).
  • Bookings with Pending or Expired status cannot be cancelled — no payment was collected, so no refund applies.
  • Cancellations after the departure time are not allowed under any circumstances.
  • If the bus operator or BusMate cancels your booking, you receive a 100% full refund regardless of timing.
  • Partially used tickets (e.g. boarded but got off early) are not eligible for any refund.

🔄 Admin-Initiated Cancellations

If BusMate or an operator cancels your booking for any reason (schedule change, bus breakdown, route suspension), you will receive a full 100% refund on your fare within 3–5 working days. You will be notified by email.
