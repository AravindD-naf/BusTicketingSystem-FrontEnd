We respect your privacy. Here's exactly what data we collect and how we use it.

Last updated: March 1, 2026

1. Who We Are

BusMate Technologies Pvt. Ltd. ("BusMate", "we", "us") operates the BusMate bus ticketing platform available at busmate.in and through our mobile applications. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you register, we collect your full name, email address, phone number, and a hashed password. We never store your password in plain text.

Booking Information

When you make a booking, we collect passenger names, ages, seat preferences, boarding and drop points, and contact details provided for that booking.

Payment Information

We do not store card numbers or full payment details. Payments are processed through PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways. We only store transaction IDs and payment status.

Usage Data

We collect information about how you use our platform including pages visited, searches performed, and booking history — to improve our services.

Device & Technical Data

We collect IP addresses, browser type, device identifiers, and operating system information for security and troubleshooting purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and confirm your bus bookings
  • To send booking confirmations and e-tickets
  • To process payments and issue refunds
  • To provide customer support
  • To send important service updates and alerts
  • To improve our platform based on usage patterns
  • To detect and prevent fraud and abuse
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

4. Data Sharing

We share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • Bus Operators: We share passenger names and contact details with the bus operator for the specific journey booked.
  • Payment Processors: Payment data is shared with our payment gateway partners strictly for transaction processing.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including 256-bit SSL encryption for all data in transit, bcrypt hashing for passwords, secure database storage with restricted access, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@busmate.in.

7. Cookies

We use cookies for session management, analytics, and improving user experience. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

8. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Booking history is retained for 7 years for legal and accounting purposes. You can request account deletion at any time — we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

9. Contact

For privacy-related queries, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@busmate.in or write to us at BusMate Technologies Pvt. Ltd., Anna Salai, Chennai – 600 002, Tamil Nadu, India.
