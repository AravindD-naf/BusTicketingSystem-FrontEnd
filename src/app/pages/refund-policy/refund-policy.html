💰 Refund Eligibility

A refund is issued only when a confirmed (paid) booking is cancelled. Bookings that were never paid (Pending or Expired status) are not eligible for a refund since no payment was collected.

Booking Status at Cancellation Refund Eligible?
Confirmed (payment completed) Yes — as per cancellation policy
Pending (payment not completed) No — no payment was made
Expired (payment window lapsed) No — no payment was made
Cancelled by operator / BusMate Yes — 100% full refund

Refunds are credited back to the original payment method used at the time of booking. We do not issue refunds to a different account or instrument.

📊 Refund Calculation

The refund amount is calculated on the base seat fare only. The following are non-refundable in all cases:

  • Convenience fee (₹20 per booking)
  • GST & applicable taxes

Example

If you paid ₹1,345 (₹1,250 fare + ₹75 GST + ₹20 fee) and cancel 30 hours before departure (75% refund tier):

  • Refund = 75% × ₹1,250 = ₹937.50
  • Cancellation fee = 25% × ₹1,250 = ₹312.50
  • GST and convenience fee (₹95) are non-refundable

🔍 How to Track Your Refund

You can track the status of your refund directly in the app:

  • Go to My Bookings
  • Find the cancelled booking
  • The refund status (Pending / Completed) is shown on the booking card

Statuses: Pending → being processed | Completed → credited to your account | Rejected → not eligible (contact support)

📞 Refund Issues?

If your refund hasn't arrived within the stated timeline, email us at support@busmate.in with your Booking ID and payment transaction reference. We'll investigate and resolve it within 48 hours.
