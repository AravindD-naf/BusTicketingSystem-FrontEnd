Step by Step

How BusMate Works

01

Search Your Route

Enter your origin city, destination, and travel date. BusMate instantly scans hundreds of buses across verified operators and shows you every available option — sorted by price, departure time, or duration.

✓ Real-time availability ✓ Multiple operators compared ✓ Filter by bus type & timing
02

Choose Your Bus & Seat

Pick the bus that suits you. Then select your preferred seat on our interactive seat map — see exactly which seats are available, locked, or already booked. You can select up to 6 seats per booking.

✓ Interactive seat map ✓ AC / Non-AC / Sleeper options ✓ Up to 6 seats per booking
03

Fill Passenger Details

Enter passenger names, ages, and contact details. Your selected seats are locked for 5 minutes while you fill in details — so nobody can grab them while you're booking.

✓ Seats locked for 5 minutes ✓ Boarding & drop point selection ✓ Contact details for e-ticket
04

Review Your Booking

Check all trip details — route, date, seat numbers, passenger info, and fare breakdown — before you pay. No surprises, no hidden fees. See exactly what you're paying for.

✓ Full fare transparency ✓ Edit if anything is wrong ✓ Cancellation policy shown upfront
05

Pay Securely

Complete payment using UPI, credit/debit card, net banking, or digital wallets. All transactions are encrypted with 256-bit SSL. Your booking is confirmed the moment payment goes through.

✓ UPI, Cards, Net Banking ✓ 256-bit SSL encryption ✓ Instant confirmation
06

Board & Travel

Your e-ticket is ready in My Bookings. Show it on your phone at boarding — no printout needed. Track your bus live, and rate your experience after the journey to help other travellers.

✓ Digital e-ticket ✓ Live bus tracking ✓ Post-trip rating
