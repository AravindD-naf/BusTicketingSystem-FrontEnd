Onboarding Process

Get Listed in 4 Simple Steps

01

Submit Your Application

Fill in the partner registration form below with your fleet details, operating routes, and contact information. No commitment required at this stage.

02

Verification & Documentation

Our team will review your application and verify your operator licence, vehicle registration, and insurance documents. This typically takes 2–3 working days.

03

Route & Schedule Setup

Once approved, our onboarding team helps you set up your routes, schedules, fares, and seat configurations in the BusMate admin system.

04

Go Live & Start Earning

Your routes go live on the BusMate platform and you start receiving bookings immediately. Earnings are settled weekly to your registered bank account.
