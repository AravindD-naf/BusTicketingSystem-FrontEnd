FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a bus ticket on BusMate?
Search for your route by entering origin, destination, and date. Choose a bus, select your seats, fill in passenger details, and complete payment. Your e-ticket is instantly available in My Bookings.
How long are seats locked during booking?
Once you select seats and click "Proceed", your chosen seats are locked exclusively for you for 5 minutes. If payment is not completed within 5 minutes, the seats are automatically released back to the pool.
What payment methods does BusMate accept?
We accept UPI, Credit Cards, Debit Cards, Net Banking, and Digital Wallets. All payments are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.
Can I cancel my booking and get a refund?
Yes, you can cancel any confirmed booking before the departure time. The refund amount depends on how early you cancel — more than 48 hours before departure gets a full refund, while closer to departure has a cancellation fee. See our full Cancellation Policy for details.
How long does a refund take?
Refunds are typically processed within 5–7 working days to your original payment method. UPI refunds are usually faster (1–3 days). You can track your refund status in My Bookings.
I completed payment but didn't get a booking confirmation. What should I do?
First check My Bookings — your booking may already be confirmed there. If the money was deducted but no booking shows, contact us at support@busmate.in with your transaction ID. We'll resolve it within 24 hours.
Can I change my seat after booking?
Seat changes are not supported after confirmation. You can cancel the existing booking (subject to cancellation policy) and make a new booking with your preferred seats.
How do I get my e-ticket?
Your e-ticket is available immediately after booking under My Bookings. You can also print it using the "Print Ticket" button on the booking confirmation page. Just show it on your phone at boarding — no printout required.
What if the bus is late or doesn't show up?
Bus punctuality is the operator's responsibility. If a bus is significantly delayed or cancelled by the operator, you are eligible for a full refund. Contact our support team immediately with your booking ID.
Can I book tickets for someone else?
Yes. When filling in passenger details, enter the traveller's name and contact information. The booking will be linked to your account but the ticket will be in the traveller's name.
