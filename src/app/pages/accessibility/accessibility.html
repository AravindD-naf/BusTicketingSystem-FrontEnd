BusMate is committed to making bus booking accessible to every Indian traveller.

Our Commitment

Accessibility for Everyone

We believe digital services should work for everyone — regardless of ability, device, or connection speed. We are actively working to meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA accessibility standards.

🎨

Colour Contrast

All text on our platform meets a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 against its background, ensuring readability for users with low vision or colour blindness.

⌨️

Keyboard Navigation

All interactive elements — buttons, forms, and links — are fully accessible using keyboard navigation only, without requiring a mouse.

🔤

Scalable Text

Text resizes cleanly up to 200% without loss of content or functionality, supporting users who need larger text sizes.

📱

Mobile Responsive

Our platform works on all screen sizes from small phones to large desktops, adapting layout and touch targets for comfortable use.

🖼️

Alternative Text

Images and icons include descriptive alternative text so screen reader users receive equivalent information to visual users.

⚡

Low-Bandwidth Friendly

Our platform is optimised for slow connections. Essential booking functions work even on 2G networks common in rural India.

Physical Accessibility

Support for Passengers with Disabilities

We work with our bus operator partners to promote physically accessible travel options.

🪑

Priority Seating

When booking, select lower-deck seats for easier boarding. Look for operators that offer designated priority seats.

🧓

Senior Citizen Friendly

We recommend lower-berth and front-of-bus seats for elderly passengers. Contact our support team for assistance with seat selection.

🤱

Travelling with Children

Book adjacent seats for family groups using our seat map. Contact support if you need help arranging family seating.

♿

Wheelchair Users

Please contact us in advance at support@busmate.in — we will coordinate with the operator to ensure appropriate boarding assistance.

📣 Known Limitations & Roadmap

We acknowledge that our platform does not yet fully meet all accessibility standards. Current known limitations include:

  • Some modal dialogs do not fully trap focus for screen reader users (in progress)
  • The interactive seat map currently relies on colour to convey status (a text label will be added)
  • Language support is currently English only (regional language support planned)

We are actively working to address these issues in upcoming releases.

💬 Feedback & Accessibility Support

If you experience any accessibility barrier while using BusMate, please contact us at accessibility@busmate.in. We aim to respond to all accessibility feedback within 5 working days and will prioritise fixes that affect core booking functionality.
